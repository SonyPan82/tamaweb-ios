# Tamaweb Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Tamaweb is a virtual pet game for iPhone.

## 1. Data We Collect

Tamaweb stores most game data locally on your device, including:

- pet progress
- settings
- save data
- local gameplay state

If you use online features, Tamaweb may process:

- a user identifier generated for your game profile
- your chosen username, if you set one
- pet-related profile data needed for online features, such as pet name, sprite, accessories, and limited interaction data

## 2. How We Use Data

We use this data only to:

- provide online gameplay features
- display pets and friend-related features
- support gameplay interactions between users
- maintain core app functionality

## 3. Data Not Used for Tracking

Tamaweb does not use your data for:

- cross-app tracking
- advertising personalization
- data broker activity

## 4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, Tamaweb may schedule local notifications on your device for gameplay reminders.

These notifications are generated on-device and are not used for advertising or tracking.

## 5. Data Sharing

Tamaweb does not sell personal data.

Online gameplay data may be processed through service providers strictly to operate the app's online functionality.

## 6. Data Storage

Most game progress is stored locally on your device.

Online feature data is stored or transmitted only as needed to provide those features.

## 7. Your Choices

You can:

- use the app without enabling notifications
- choose whether to set a username
- reset your game data from inside the app

## 8. Children's Privacy

Tamaweb is a game and does not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is reasonably necessary to provide gameplay functionality.
